About Diploma Dost

Welcome to Diploma Dost, your one-stop destination for all your MSBTE (Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education) diploma study resources. We understand the challenges that diploma students face in accessing relevant and reliable study materials, and that’s why we created this platform to cater specifically to the needs of MSBTE diploma students.

Our Mission

At Diploma Dost, our mission is to empower MSBTE diploma students by providing them easy access to a wide range of study resources. We aim to facilitate seamless learning by offering comprehensive PDFs of books & notes, Lab manuals, the latest K-Scheme syllabus, previous year question papers, model answer papers, video playlist and much more.

Why Choose Diploma Dost

We take pride in curating an extensive collection of study materials to meet the diverse needs of MSBTE diploma students. Whether you’re looking for textbooks, solved manuals, or the latest syllabus, you’ll find it all here.
User-Friendly Interface: Our website is designed with you in mind. We prioritize user experience, ensuring that you can navigate the site effortlessly and find the resources you need without any hassle.
Reliable and Updated Content: Keeping pace with the dynamic nature of educational content, we regularly update our resources to provide you with the latest study materials. Our aim is to help you stay ahead in your academic journey.

How We Can Help You:
  • K-Scheme Syllabus: Access the latest K-Scheme syllabus for your diploma program to stay informed about the curriculum
  • PDFs of Books & Notes: Download PDFs of relevant textbooks & Notes to enhance your understanding of the subjects covered in your course.
  • Previous Year Question Papers: Practice with confidence by accessing previous year question papers in PDF format.
  • Model Answer Paper: Helps for understanding the concpet of better answering in diploma exams
  • Video Playlist: Rnowned video lectures from YouTube for helping with topics of each Subjects
